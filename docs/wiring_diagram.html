GPS sleep is handled in software. Before entering deep sleep, the firmware sends the ATGM336H a $PMTK161,0 backup-mode command over UART, which drops its draw from ~25 mA to roughly 10–50 µA while VCC stays applied. No hardware power-control pin is required.

2. Battery Voltage Divider

  • Two matching 100kΩ through 220 kΩ resistors in series between BAT+ and GND halve the battery voltage.
  • Midpoint tap feeds D10 / A10 (GPIO9), within the ESP32 ADC range.
  • High resistance = negligible drain (~10 µA).
  • BAT− is the same net as GND — you can run the divider directly across the battery terminals.
